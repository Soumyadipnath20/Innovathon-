Reusable Items

Here are top 4 items that can be rescued from the rubbish and reused again

Plastic Bottles and Plastic Bags

  • Plastic bags can be used as shelves and garbage liners.
  • Plastic bottles can be reused as cooking tools,rainwater catcher,planters.

Newspapers,Shoe Boxes,Egg Cartons

  • Egg catons can used to start seedlings, store jewelry, make a palette for paints, or to organize craft supplies.
  • Shoes boxes can be used as as drawer dividers, closet organizers, shoe storage, or cord organizers. You can even use the cardboard for firestarter or packing.
  • Newspaper can be used as wrapping paper in your cat litter boxes, to clean windows with them, or as a firestarter. You can even use them to start seeds for your garden.

Used Tea Bags

  • Used Tea Bags instead of throwing away in trash bins can used for relieving puffy eyes, adding them to your bath, soothing insect bites and burns, and feeding your garden.

Wine Bottles

  • Empty wine bottles can be used as tikki torches, a privacy wall, bird feeders, or even as a pendant light. Paint them and make them into vases.
