How to Recycle Properly

Recycling Process

Recycling helps save the environment by reducing waste. Learn how to recycle different materials properly.

Step 1: Collect waste → Step 2: Sort materials → Step 3: Process recyclables → Step 4: Reuse in new products.

Plastic

Rinse plastic bottles and containers before recycling. Avoid plastic bags unless specified by your local recycling program. Some plastics can be repurposed into new bottles, clothing, or construction materials. Check the recycling symbol and number on plastic items to ensure proper disposal.

Paper

Flatten cardboard boxes and remove food stains before recycling paper. Newspapers, magazines, and office papers can be processed into new paper products. Shredded paper should be placed in a paper bag before recycling to prevent scattering.

Glass

Separate glass by color. Rinse before placing in the bin. Recycled glass can be melted and reshaped into new bottles and jars multiple times. Avoid mixing glass with ceramics or tempered glass, as these cannot be recycled together.

Metal

Recycle aluminum cans, tins, and scrap metal after cleaning them. Metals can be reprocessed into car parts, appliances, and new cans. Ensure that aerosol cans are completely empty before recycling.
